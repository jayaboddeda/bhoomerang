FAQ

Questions we hear often.

What is AEO and why does my Hyderabad business need it?
AEO stands for Answer Engine Optimisation — structuring your content so search engines surface it in featured snippets, People Also Ask boxes, and voice search answers. As customers in Hyderabad increasingly search conversationally, AEO makes your business the answer they see, even before they click.
What is GEO and is it relevant in Hyderabad?
GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimisation — making your brand visible to AI tools like Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT and Perplexity. Yes, highly relevant: search behaviour is shifting toward AI-generated answers, and GEO keeps your brand in those answers.
How long does SEO take to show results?
Local SEO improvements typically show measurable results within 3–6 months. Competitive national keywords can take 6–12 months. AEO and GEO wins can appear faster — some featured-snippet optimisations are reflected within weeks.
Do you guarantee first-page rankings?
No reputable SEO agency can — Google decides rankings. What we guarantee is process: the right work, on the right schedule, measured transparently every month.
